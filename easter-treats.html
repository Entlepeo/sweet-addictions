Soft Pretzels

üçØ Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 2 tbsp baking soda
  • Coarse salt for topping

üç¥ Instructions

  1. Activate yeast by dissolving it in warm water with sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine flour, salt, and yeast mixture. Knead for 5 minutes until dough is smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F (220¬∞C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  5. Divide dough into 8 portions, roll each into a rope, and shape into pretzels.
  6. Boil water with baking soda, then dip pretzels into the water for 30 seconds.
  7. Place on the baking sheet, sprinkle with coarse salt, and bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
