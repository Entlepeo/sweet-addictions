Frozen Desserts

Cool and creamy treats for hot days

Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

30 mins + freezing 1 quart Medium
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 6 large egg yolks
Instructions:
  1. 1Heat cream, milk, 1/2 cup sugar, and salt in saucepan until steaming
  2. 2Whisk egg yolks with remaining 1/4 cup sugar until pale
  3. 3Slowly whisk hot cream mixture into egg yolks
  4. 4Return mixture to saucepan and cook over medium-low until thickened (175°F)
  5. 5Strain through fine-mesh sieve into clean bowl
  6. 6Stir in vanilla extract
  7. 7Chill mixture completely (at least 4 hours or overnight)
  8. 8Churn in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions
  9. 9Transfer to airtight container and freeze until firm (4+ hours)
Total chilling/freezing time: 8+ hours

Raspberry Sorbet

20 mins + freezing 1 pint Easy
Ingredients:
  • 3 cups fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Pinch of salt
Instructions:
  1. 1Combine sugar and water in saucepan, heat until sugar dissolves
  2. 2Cool syrup completely
  3. 3Puree raspberries in blender until smooth
  4. 4Strain through fine-mesh sieve to remove seeds
  5. 5Mix raspberry puree with cooled syrup, lemon juice, and salt
  6. 6Chill mixture for at least 2 hours
  7. 7Churn in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions
  8. 8Transfer to airtight container and freeze until firm (4+ hours)
Total chilling/freezing time: 6+ hours

Honey Vanilla Frozen Yogurt

15 mins + freezing 1 quart Easy
Ingredients:
  • 3 cups Greek yogurt (full-fat)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp vodka or liqueur (optional, for creamier texture)
Instructions:
  1. 1Whisk together yogurt, honey, vanilla, and salt until smooth
  2. 2Add vodka if using (helps prevent ice crystals)
  3. 3Chill mixture for at least 1 hour
  4. 4Churn in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions
  5. 5Transfer to airtight container and freeze until firm (4+ hours)
Total chilling/freezing time: 5+ hours

Authentic Italian Gelato

45 mins + freezing 1 quart Advanced
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup skim milk powder
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 vanilla bean or 2 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions:
  1. 1Heat milk and cream in saucepan until steaming (do not boil)
  2. 2If using vanilla bean, split and scrape seeds into milk mixture
  3. 3Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick
  4. 4Whisk in milk powder, cornstarch, and salt
  5. 5Slowly whisk hot milk mixture into egg mixture
  6. 6Return to saucepan and cook over medium-low until thickened (185°F)
  7. 7Strain through fine-mesh sieve into clean bowl
  8. 8If using vanilla extract, stir it in now
  9. 9Cool to room temperature, then chill completely (4+ hours)
  10. 10Churn in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions
  11. 11Transfer to airtight container and freeze until firm (2-3 hours)
Total chilling/freezing time: 6+ hours
