Blue Christmas Truffles

Prep Time: 20 mins + chill | Yield: 18 truffles

Tools Needed: Mixing bowl, spatula, baking sheet, parchment paper

Ingredients:
  • 8 oz white chocolate, chopped
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Blue sprinkles or sanding sugar
Instructions:
  1. Melt white chocolate in a heatproof bowl over simmering water.
  2. Beat cream cheese until smooth, then stir in the melted chocolate and vanilla.
  3. Chill mixture until firm enough to scoop (about 1 hour).
  4. Form into balls and roll in blue sprinkles.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Eggnog Panna Cotta with Spiked Cranberry Sauce

Prep Time: 25 mins + chill | Yield: 4 servings

Tools Needed: Saucepan, whisk, ramekins

Ingredients:

Panna Cotta:

  • 1 cup eggnog
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 envelope unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Cranberry Sauce:

  • 1 cup cranberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp rum or bourbon (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Sprinkle gelatin over 2 tbsp cold water; let sit 5 mins.
  2. Heat eggnog, cream, sugar, and nutmeg in a saucepan until hot, but not boiling.
  3. Stir in gelatin until dissolved. Pour into ramekins and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
  4. For sauce, simmer cranberries, sugar, and water until thickened. Stir in rum.
  5. Serve panna cotta topped with cranberry sauce.
Santa Claus Macarons

Prep Time: 1 hour | Yield: 24 macarons

Tools Needed: Mixer, piping bag, baking mat

Ingredients:

Macaron Shells:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Red food coloring

Filling:

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
Instructions:
  1. Sift almond flour and powdered sugar together.
  2. Whip egg whites until foamy; add sugar gradually and beat until stiff peaks form. Add food coloring.
  3. Fold in dry mixture. Pipe rounds onto parchment and rest 30 mins.
  4. Bake at 300°F (150°C) for 15 mins. Cool completely.
  5. Mix filling ingredients and pipe onto one shell, then sandwich with another.
  6. Decorate shells with icing to resemble Santa faces.
Eggnog Cheesecake

Prep Time: 30 mins | Yield: 12 slices

Tools Needed: Springform pan, mixer, whisk

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 8-oz packages cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup eggnog
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions:
  1. Mix graham crumbs and butter, then press into bottom of pan. Bake at 325°F for 10 mins.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs, one at a time, then eggnog, nutmeg, and vanilla.
  3. Pour into crust and bake at 325°F for 55 mins.
  4. Cool and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
Peppermint Bark

Prep Time: 15 mins | Yield: 12 servings

Tools Needed: Baking sheet, parchment paper

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb dark chocolate
  • 1/2 lb white chocolate
  • 1/2 cup crushed peppermint candies
Instructions:
  1. Melt dark chocolate and spread onto baking sheet.
  2. Melt white chocolate and drizzle over dark chocolate.
  3. Sprinkle with crushed peppermint candies.
  4. Let set in fridge for 1 hour, then break into pieces.
