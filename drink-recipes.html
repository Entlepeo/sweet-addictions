Mocktails & Cocktails

Refreshing drink recipes for every occasion

Virgin Mojito

10 mins 1 serving Easy
Ingredients:
  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 1 cup club soda
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint sprig for garnish
Instructions:
  1. 1Muddle mint leaves, lime wedges, and sugar in a glass
  2. 2Fill the glass with ice cubes
  3. 3Pour club soda over the ice
  4. 4Stir well and garnish with mint sprig

Strawberry Daiquiri

5 mins 2 servings Easy
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1/2 cup sparkling water
  • Lime slices for garnish
Instructions:
  1. 1Combine strawberries, lime juice, and sugar in blender
  2. 2Add ice and blend until smooth
  3. 3Pour into glasses and top with sparkling water
  4. 4Garnish with lime slices

Virgin Pina Colada

5 mins 2 servings Easy
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Pineapple wedge and cherry for garnish
Instructions:
  1. 1Combine all ingredients in blender
  2. 2Blend until smooth and creamy
  3. 3Pour into chilled glasses
  4. 4Garnish with pineapple and cherry

Chocolate Martini

7 mins 1 serving Medium
Ingredients:
  • 1 oz chocolate syrup
  • 1 oz heavy cream
  • 4 oz milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes
  • Chocolate shavings for garnish
Instructions:
  1. 1Rim martini glass with chocolate syrup
  2. 2Combine all ingredients in shaker with ice
  3. 3Shake vigorously for 30 seconds
  4. 4Strain into prepared glass
  5. 5Garnish with chocolate shavings
