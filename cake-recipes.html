Cake Recipes

Decadent cakes for every celebration

Classic Red Velvet Cake

1 hour 30 mins 3-layer cake Intermediate
Ingredients:
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened (frosting)
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened (frosting)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar (frosting)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (frosting)
Instructions:
  1. 1Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 9-inch cake pans.
  2. 2Whisk together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. 3In another bowl, beat oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar.
  4. 4Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  5. 5Divide batter evenly between prepared pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. 6Cool cakes in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  7. 7For frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla.
  8. 8Frost cooled cake layers and decorate as desired.

Blackberry Lavender Cake

1 hour 45 mins 3-layer cake Advanced
Ingredients:
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tbsp dried culinary lavender
  • 2 cups fresh blackberries
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups heavy cream (frosting)
  • 8 oz cream cheese (frosting)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (frosting)
  • 1/2 cup blackberry jam (filling)
Instructions:
  1. 1Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line three 8-inch cake pans.
  2. 2Heat milk with lavender until steaming. Let steep for 30 minutes, then strain.
  3. 3Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. 4Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. 5Alternate adding dry ingredients and lavender milk to butter mixture.
  6. 6Fold in lemon zest and vanilla. Gently fold in 1 cup mashed blackberries.
  7. 7Divide batter between pans and bake for 25-28 minutes.
  8. 8For frosting: Whip cream cheese, then add cream and powdered sugar.
  9. 9Assemble cake with blackberry jam between layers and frost.
  10. 10Garnish with fresh blackberries and lavender sprigs.

Classic Vanilla Cake

1 hour 2-layer cake Easy
Ingredients:
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened (frosting)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar (frosting)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (frosting)
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract (frosting)
Instructions:
  1. 1Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. 2Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. 3Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy (about 5 minutes).
  4. 4Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. 5Mix in vanilla extract.
  6. 6Alternate adding flour mixture and milk, beginning and ending with flour.
  7. 7Divide batter between pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. 8Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  9. 9For frosting: Beat butter until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar.
  10. 10Add cream and vanilla, beat until light and fluffy.
  11. 11Frost cooled cake layers and decorate as desired.

New York Cheesecake

6 hours (includes chilling) 9-inch cheesecake Intermediate
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (crust)
  • 32 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
Instructions:
  1. 1Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Wrap a 9-inch springform pan with foil.
  2. 2Mix crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press firmly into pan bottom.
  3. 3Bake crust for 10 minutes, then cool.
  4. 4Beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add sugar.
  5. 5Add eggs one at a time, mixing just until combined.
  6. 6Mix in sour cream, heavy cream, vanilla, salt, and flour.
  7. 7Pour filling over crust. Place pan in a larger baking dish.
  8. 8Add hot water to larger dish until halfway up springform pan.
  9. 9Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes until edges are set but center jiggles slightly.
  10. 10Turn off oven, leave door slightly open, and let cheesecake cool for 1 hour.
  11. 11Remove from oven and chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
