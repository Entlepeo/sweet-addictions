Sweet-Chocolate Killer Cookies

🍯 Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg

📝 Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Mix in egg and vanilla.
  4. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt in another bowl.
  5. Combine dry and wet ingredients.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips and marshmallows.
  7. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes.
  9. Cool before serving.

⏳ Prep Time

15 minutes

⏰ Cook Time

10 minutes

🍴 Servings

24 cookies

🛠️ Tools

  • Baking sheet
  • Mixing bowls
  • Measuring spoons
  • Cooling rack

Spooky Halloween Brownies

🍯 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

📝 Instructions

  1. Preheat to 350°F (175°C), grease pan.
  2. Mix butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Whisk dry ingredients separately, then combine.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Pour into pan and bake 20–25 mins.
  6. Cool, cut, serve.

⏳ Prep Time

15 minutes

⏰ Cook Time

25 minutes

🍴 Servings

16 brownies

🛠️ Tools

  • Baking pan
  • Mixing bowls
  • Measuring spoons
  • Cooling rack

Frankenstein Pudding Cups

🍯 Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 pkg instant chocolate pudding
  • 1.5 cups whipped cream
  • Green food coloring
  • Chocolate chip eyes
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Black frosting

📝 Instructions

  1. Whisk milk and pudding mix. Chill 5 mins.
  2. Fold in whipped cream, add coloring.
  3. Fill cups halfway. Add marshmallows for hair.
  4. Decorate face with chips and frosting. Chill before serving.

⏳ Prep Time

10 minutes

⏰ Cook Time

5 minutes

🍴 Servings

4 servings

🛠️ Tools

  • Whisk
  • Mixing bowls
  • Cups
  • Refrigerator
